Where Barbarism Becomes Modern Art

You know that "good old days" talk? In Highlander, we see ancient Scotland and 1986 New York slapping each other in the face. On one side, kilt-wearing warriors literally chopping off heads! On the other, the concrete jungle of the 80s, with its flying cars and folks at the peak of wild capitalism. And what's that got to do with you, young Wi-Fi generation? Everything!
